Caltrain Ridership

Last updated: 2023-02-21

This site contains graphs and a table of Caltrain's average weekday ridership from Jul 2017 to Dec 2022 sourced from Caltrain Board of Directors meeting agendas. Average weekday ridership is reported with a 1-month lag, so the most recent data is from Dec 2022.

You can find a CSV of Caltrain's average weekday ridership and the script to generate this site on GitHub. You can find more of my work on my website, echevarria.io.

Why I made this

It's surprisingly difficult to get a simple graph or table of Caltrain ridership numbers. Caltrain's official ridership page has links to three different sources, only one of which, the Caltrain Board of Directors monthly meeting agendas, is up-to-date. Each agenda PDF has at most a year of ridership numbers, but some only have the actual average weekday ridership number for that specific month. The format has changed several times, so it's not even doable to programmatically extract ridership numbers. It shouldn't be this hard.

It took effort to collect these numbers in the real world â€“ distributing them in a reasonable way should be the easy part. If Caltrain won't, I will.
